Inspire.

Boldness.


At Bolder Learning Design, we believe in the potential of people.
We believe that when people are empowered to take risks, the sky is the limit. Success happens when you are more confident, bolder - ready to grow. We believe that building relationships while learning has positive impacts on communities and societies. For us, an unquestionable piece of helping humans become bolder is by designing the experiences that make it possible. Want in?



Every learning journey has a purpose
Each learning experience is unique and deserving of a comprehensive strategy that fits its purpose. We come in where you need us, and focus on the areas of the experience that benefit from our custom plans of action, tailored to your goals and assessment of success.


Driving learning through pedagogy + content + growth.
We feel that our learning environments should not only be a place to measure growth, but also be a source of delight. Creating accurate learner personas & problem solving around a their specific motivations & frustrations is critical for success. We believe spending time refining learning outcomes and differentiating activities is crucial to creating equitable learning opportunities. Our designs are founded on best practices in pedagogy, not gimmicks, to build authentic engagement.
